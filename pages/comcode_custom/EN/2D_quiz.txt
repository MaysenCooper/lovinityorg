[semihtml][title]2D. Section Quiz and Rules quiz[/title]
Congratulations! You have made it to your first two quizzes. Please be sure to carefully read and answer the questions. You may look back at any documents. Please send your answers to the staff member responsible for your training.<br />
<br />
<strong>Please answer these questions in your own words.&nbsp;</strong>Copying / pasting text from the documents as part of your answers will automatically result in a wrong answer.

<h2>Section Quiz</h2>

<ol>
	<li>Failure to uphold the non-disclosure agreement can result in what?</li>
	<li>For how long does the non-disclosure agreement contract last?</li>
	<li>If you are unsure of something during your training or staff time, what should you do?</li>
	<li>What training is mandatory for all staff and teaches new staff on how to promote a safe and welcoming environment?</li>
	<li>What is the name of the website software that The Lovinity Community+ uses?</li>
	<li>Name one significant difference between high-level administrators and low-level administrators / advisers.</li>
	<li>Which of the staff teams is responsible for giving advice and support to other members as they have been selected as trusted individuals?</li>
	<li>How many days prior notice are you expected to give if you decide you wish to depart staff?</li>
	<li>If you depart staff, but later decide you want to return, how many days since you departed must you wait before you may apply again?</li>
	<li>Why do we ask that you have fun and enjoy yourself during your time on staff?</li>
</ol>
In order to pass this quiz, you will need to score an 80% or higher on the above 10 questions. If you fail to do so, you&#39;ll be asked to re-read the documents in sections 1 and 2 and re-take the quiz.

<h2>Rules Quiz</h2>
By now, you should have carefully read all of our policy documents. If you have not, now&#39;s the time to do so before you begin the rules quiz. This quiz will cover material from the <a href="https://lovinity.org/forum/rules.htm">rules</a>, <a class="user_link" href="https://lovinity.org/tos.htm">Terms of Use</a>,&nbsp;and <a href="https://lovinity.org/privacy.htm">Privacy Policy</a>&nbsp;. This quiz will NOT cover content from the Safe Space Policy; that is covered in the Safe Space training quizzes.

<ol>
	<li>The rules, Terms of Use, Safe Space Policy, and Privacy Policy apply to which groups of people: Guests, members, trainees, staff, administrators? More than one answer may apply.</li>
	<li>Fill in the blank: Because of the Children&#39;s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), members under the age of 13 must (blank) to be allowed to register on The Lovinity Community+.</li>
	<li>When is pornography and excessive gore okay on The Lovinity Community+?</li>
	<li>Give an example, other than the one on the rules, of&nbsp;what is NOT acceptable discussion for drugs, tobacco, alcohol, weapons, gambling, or products made from endangered species.</li>
	<li>When is it okay to post private content in public locations?</li>
	<li>In your own brief words, describe the disciplinary process.</li>
	<li>If a user is allowed to appeal discipline, they must do so within how many hours?</li>
	<li>Are members permitted to access our website using a proxy server?</li>
	<li>How often must a member log in to be considered an active member, and therefore not subject to inactive member account deletion?</li>
	<li>Name one rule violation that would result in an immediate permanent ban.</li>
	<li>How can members report content that is in violation of our policies?</li>
	<li>Does our website use cookies?</li>
	<li>If a user wishes to keep something completely private, what should they do, according to the Privacy policy?</li>
	<li>If a member does not want something they submit being indexed by search engines, what should they do?</li>
	<li>What is transmitted to the Stop Forum Spam service to check for spam activity?</li>
</ol>
To pass this quiz, you will need to score an 80% or higher (moderators in training must score 90% or higher). If you fail, you will be asked to re-read the necessary documents and then re-take this quiz.<br />
