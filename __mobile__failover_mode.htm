TLC+ Journal

Welcome to the Journal section of The Lovinity Community+!

Journal Entries
Write entries about the happenings in your life and the thoughts and emotions you experience. Choose your moods, emotions, and activities. Then write away! You can even attach files and pictures to entries.

Privacy Settings
You can control who can view your entries on a per-entry basis. Let the whole world see, let only logged in members see, or keep it to yourself.

Journals
Make multiple journals (journal categories) to separate, for example, personal life from traveling and vacationing.

Encrypted Entries
NEW! - You can have your entries encrypted in our database using AES-256 encryption. To encrypt content, see this FAQ entry

Search
COMING SOON - Search for journals in multiple ways: by date, mood level, emotions experienced, and activities participated in.

Emotion Tracking
COMING SOON - Track your emotions and trends over time.

Activity Correlation
COMING SOON - See which activities you tend to participate in depending on your level of mood.
